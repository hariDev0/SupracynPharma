Services

We cater to various Corporate Hospital, leading Pharmacies, Chain Pharmacies, Institutions, individual patient supplies etc. As referred above, we cater to almost all the segments of pharmaceutical distribution as stated below.

Retail pharmacy

We supply to leading pharmacies spread across the city with specialised products. Apart from our counter sales and supplies with certain areas through booking agents.

Retails chain pharmacies

We supply to centralised purchase of various pharmacies such as Hetro, Medplus, Keimed etc.

Corporate hospitals

We supply to various corporate hospitals as per the agreed rates and terms of the respective companies.

Govt. hospitals

We regularly participate in the tenders floated by the Govt. Hospitals under Rate contracts and local purchases, apart from emergency supplies.

Semi Govt. Organizations

We even supply to various Semi Govt. Organizations such as BEHL, HAL, Indian Airlines, The Singarnei Collieries Co. Ltd, South Central Railways under the Annual Supplies and Local Purchase.

Rate Contracts

We supply to various Govt. Organisations under single vendor rate contract where all the items required by the institution are supplied by us at tender discount for the period of the contract.

Exports

We work as a sourcing partner to our International clients fulfilling the needs of sourcing for them in respect of high quality generics as per their request.
